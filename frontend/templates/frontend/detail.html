About Clash Of Clans

Clash of Clans (COC) is a mobile strategy game developed and published by Supercell. In the game, players build and defend their own villages and attack other players’ villages to gain resources, trophies, and territory. Players can join or create clans, allowing them to work with other players and participate in clan wars.
